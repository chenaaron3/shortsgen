title: The Right to Happiness

--- HOOK ---
The secret to happiness isn't what you think.
--- BODY ---
We often chase happiness in things or achievements, but what if it's simpler? The Dalai Lama suggests focusing on what brings joy and removing what causes suffering. Yet, many struggle, trapped by negative habits and societal pressures. These emotions can be deeply rooted, making it hard to shift.
--- CLOSE ---
Start small: train your mind to spot one positive thought each day. Happiness grows from there.
