title: Identity and Habit Change

--- HOOK ---
Want to change your habits? You're probably focusing on the wrong thing.
--- BODY ---
Most people aim at outcomes or tweak their routines.
But real change goes deeper. It starts with how you see yourself.
Consider this: A person who sees themselves as "healthy" naturally adopts healthy habits.
Their identity drives their actions, not the other way around.
--- CLOSE ---
To transform a habit, start by asking: "What kind of person do I want to be?"
