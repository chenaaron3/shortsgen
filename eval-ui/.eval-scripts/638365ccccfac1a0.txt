title: Making Habits Inevitable

--- HOOK ---
Success might hinge more on breaking bad habits than building good ones.
--- BODY ---
Think about your downtime. It's easy to scroll through social media, right? These small distractions seem harmless, but they pile up. They become obstacles in your path to progress. Philosopher Alfred North Whitehead believed that progress is about managing these little habits that hold us back.
--- CLOSE ---
Identify one bad habit today. Make it harder to do. Watch how success becomes easier.
