title: Social Influence on Habits

--- HOOK ---
Your friends might be making you gain weight.
--- BODY ---
Imagine this: a study tracked twelve thousand people for thirty-two years. If a friend became obese, your chance of gaining weight jumped by 57 percent. It's not just coincidence. When one partner loses weight, the other often follows. Invisible peer pressure is powerful. We imitate those we admire, often without realizing it.
--- CLOSE ---
Look around. Are your relationships shaping your habits? Choose your influences wisely.
