Chapter 1: Rich Dad, Poor Dad

The chapter introduces the reader to two father figures, "Poor Dad" and "Rich Dad," illustrating their distinct financial philosophies. "Poor Dad" values job security and a steady income, while "Rich Dad" emphasizes the importance of financial education and entrepreneurial thinking.

Kiyosaki explores the concept of making money work for you, as opposed to working solely for money. He introduces the crucial notions of assets and liabilities, laying the groundwork for understanding wealth-building principles.

The chapter delves into the mindset needed for financial success, emphasizing the significance of financial education and a proactive approach to building wealth.

Through personal anecdotes, Kiyosaki sets the stage for readers to question conventional financial wisdom and consider alternative perspectives on money and success.
